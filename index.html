About Me

Born to Wilfred Sr. and Catherine Mbewe, Wilfred N. Mbewe is an innovative young Zambian man with a drive to someday better his country and continent at large. When he is not learning at The University of Zambia,Wilfred is often learning different skills online. From how to construct a tiny home to computer coding. He is very passionate about connecting and improving trade within Africa,as a means of empowering one another. His interests led to the formation of Tmmp.llps. A small-scale company he started with his friends to connect truck owners to people in need of delivery services. Other than being very driven and ambitious, Wilfred is compassionate,reliable and trustworthy. Such that he oversees most of his father's estates with wisdom beyond his years.
